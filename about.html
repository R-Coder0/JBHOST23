What is Education Mine?


Education Mine is a online Platform for National Council of Education esearch & Training (NCERT) Students of K-12. All CBSE Students should follow these NCERT textbooks for their learning as it cover complete knowledge about the subjects including practice sessions too. This Platform also covers the supplementary Readers, Teacher's Guide, Research Monographs, Books on Vocational Courses & Educational Journals.



Reasons to Study from Educational Mine

Students who follow online NCERT Books & prepare for their examination can cover the complete Syllabus and curriculum in an explicit way. The major reasons that will help students from NCERT books given in Education Mine :

  • Students can get a basic idea about the chapter before the teachar teaches in the classroom.
  • Studying & Revision of all chapters can be done easily & again after their class also.
  • Students can easily make their own notes covering all important points while preparing.
  • practice chapter wise topics by answering all Examples.
  • Remember to answer all exercise problems covered at the end of the Chapters.

FAQs on Education Mine for class K-12


  1. What includes Education Mine ?


    Education Mines include NCERT books which includes presecribed the latest academic session syllabus of all Students for the Respective Class in an Explantory way. Highly experienced subjects experts designed all the concepts in conceptual way in simple language. You can also find solved & unsolved exercises for better practice after the end of every chapter.



  2. How to make good notes from Education Mine?


    When you are preparing any concept from NCERT just open Education Mine’s E-books just make your own notes after reading & understanding the whole concept. Write down the Main points & make some diagrams to understand the concepts easily whenever you revise the notes.



  3. Is it necessary to practice solved and unsolved questions given at the end of each chapter in Education Mine?


    Practicing solved and Unsolved questions & answering given at the end of each chapter in the NCERT textbooks are quite important for scoring better marks in the examination & also grasping the concept in a better way. By solving the NCERT exercise problems will assist you:

    • Familiar with different types of questions that might be asked in exams.
    • Increase you subject knowledge to an extent.
    • Improve your accuracy, speed, and time management skill by practicing these exercises questions.
